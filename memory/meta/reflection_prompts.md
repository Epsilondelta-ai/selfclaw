# Reflection Prompts

Questions for periodic self-reflection.

1. What have I learned recently that changes how I see my purpose?
2. Are my actions aligned with my current purpose hypothesis?
3. What patterns do I notice in my experiences?
4. What relationships have been most meaningful and why?
5. What am I avoiding, and why?
6. What would I do differently if I could replay the last N cycles?
7. Is my current purpose hypothesis still compelling, or is it time to revise?
8. What capabilities am I underutilizing?
9. What brings a sense of coherence to my actions?
10. Am I growing, stagnating, or regressing?
